Reliable Internet Continuity with Starlink

When your internet connection is interrupted, your business faces significant challenges. The longer your service is down, the greater the impact on your operations and revenue. With Starlink Internet Continuity, you can eliminate the anxiety of costly downtime.

Starlink Internet Continuity offers a dependable backup connectivity service that ensures uninterrupted broadband access whenever your primary internet service experiences an outage. As a satellite internet solution, Starlink is shielded from the vulnerabilities associated with traditional internet providers, such as human error and environmental disasters that can affect cable, DSL, or cellular services.

How It Works

Installation: Sit back and relax! Our certified technician will handle the installation of all necessary equipment, including an Automatic Failover Router that connects to both your existing router and internet service.

Seamless Transition: In the event of an outage, the Automatic Failover Router instantly detects the issue and redirects your traffic to the satellite backup, ensuring your business remains operational. Its as if nothing ever changed!

Automatic Recovery: Once your primary internet connection is restored, the Automatic Failover Router automatically switches back to your original service. This swift and automated process keeps your business connected without requiring any manual intervention, allowing you to concentrate on what truly matters.
